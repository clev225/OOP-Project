About Us

Welcome to ANDITO Ako

Welcome to ANDITO Ako, your premier destination for seamless and efficient student-counsel engagement at SLSU (Southern Luzon State University). Our name, ANDITO Ako, is not just an acronym; it encapsulates our commitment to providing an Appoint Now, DIrect Talk Online platform exclusively designed for students.

At ANDITO Ako, we believe that mental health is just as important as physical health. We strive to create a safe and inclusive environment where students can feel comfortable sharing their thoughts and feelings. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all students, regardless of their background or location.

We are committed to providing students with the support they need to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. If you are a student looking for a safe and confidential space to talk about your mental health concerns, ANDITO Ako is here for you. Appoint now and start your journey towards a healthier and happier life.
